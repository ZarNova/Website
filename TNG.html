About Us

The Next Generation School (TNG) is a leading educational institution in Qatar, offering a dynamic learning environment that fosters academic excellence and personal development. Our approach integrates modern education with Islamic principles.

Our Curriculum

TNG School offers a well-rounded curriculum focused on academic excellence, Islamic education, and soft skills development. Our students engage in both theoretical and practical learning, preparing them for future success in a globalized world.

Admissions

Admission to TNG School is open to students from various backgrounds. We offer a thorough and comprehensive process to ensure the best fit for our students. Visit our admissions page for more information on the application process and requirements.

Our Courses

We offer a variety of courses that cater to the needs of all our students, ranging from Kindergarten through Secondary School. Our courses integrate modern education and Islamic studies, ensuring a balanced learning experience.

Careers

TNG School provides excellent opportunities for educators and staff to join our team. We value diversity and innovation and offer a supportive environment for professional growth. Visit our careers page for available job openings.
