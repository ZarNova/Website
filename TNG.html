About Us

Founded in 2009, The Next Generation (TNG) School in Qatar is committed to providing quality education in a safe and positive environment. Our mission is to foster innovation, independent learning, and active participation in co-curricular and extra-curricular activities, while upholding Islamic values.

Our Values:
- Quality: Striving for excellence
- Sincerity: Genuine actions and words
- Teamwork: Collaboration for success
- Ambition: Aspiring to be our best
- Respect: Treating others with kindness

Join us at TNG, where we turn information into knowledge and wisdom.

Our Curriculum

At The Next Generation (TNG) School, we offer a comprehensive curriculum designed to challenge, stimulate, and promote critical thinking, decision-making, and problem-solving skills. As a Cambridge International School, we prepare learners for successful IGCSE and A-Level examinations with the CAIE board.

Our curriculum includes:
- Core Subjects: English, Mathematics, Science, and Social Studies
- Language Studies: Arabic and Islamic Studies
- Technology: Computer Science and Robotics
- Soft Skills: Leadership, teamwork, and communication
- Qatar History: Understanding the rich heritage and culture of Qatar

We ensure that each learner has a wide range of learning experiences that cater to their academic and personal growth.

Admissions

Required Documents:
- Student's Qatari ID, Passport, Birth Certificate, Vaccination records, Health Card, Previous school reports
- Parent's Qatari ID and Passport

Admission Steps:
1. Submit an online application with required documents.
2. Receive confirmation and schedule an entrance test.
3. Attend the test and await results.
4. Complete the admission process upon successful test results.

For detailed information, visit our admissions page: https://tngqatar.online
